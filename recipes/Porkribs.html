Porkribs recipe

Description

This is the best pork ribs recipe!

Ingredients needed to cook these pork ribs

  • Pork ribs
  • Butter
  • Spice rub
  • oven

How to make these ribs

  1. First rub down the ribs with all the spices
  2. Put the oven at a medium-high heat and slow cook for 8 hours
  3. After tender. rest for 2 hours then serve
