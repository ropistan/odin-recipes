Sandwhich recipe

Description

This is the best sandwhich recipe!

Ingredients needed to cook this sandwhich

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Mayonaise

How to make this sandwhich

  1. First toast the bread with butter
  2. Then cook the eggs seperately with butter as well
  3. After cooking is done , assemble the sandwhich and done.
