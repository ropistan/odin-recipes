Pasta

Description

This is a pasta recipe. It is one of the most tasties dishes in the world

Ingredients needed to cook white sauce pasta

  • Pasta
    • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Eggs

Steps to make white sauce pasta

  1. Make dough by mixing flour and eggs
  2. Boil the pasta
  3. Cook the sauce by adding cheese,flour and butter
  4. Mix the pasta and sauce together, then serve.
